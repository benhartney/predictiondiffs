What is this?

This website displays predictions from various source (currently just Metaculus), to make following the news easier and clearer.

  • See multiple event predictions at once
  • See how predictions have changed - the core experience is to come here every x days, and the first thing you will see is how the predictions have changed since your last visit
  • Options to filter out smaller changes, so you can see the most important changes
  • Any prediction that is <50% gets inverted, so that you're always seeing 50%+ predictions (aka the most likely outcomes)

When we use social media for news, or go to a news website, we're doing a few things:

  1. learning what has happened
  2. getting a more visceral or emotional understanding of what has happened
  3. finding out what will probably happen in the future
  4. entertaining or distracting ourselves

This website is focused on "c. finding out what will probably happen in the future". It will give you a more accurate understanding of what will probably happen, and allows you to get that without all the other stuff in the list above.

It does this by displaying existing predictions from various sources, and displaying them in ways that make them more news-like.

If you'd like to understand more about why these types of predictions are generally accurate, see here.
